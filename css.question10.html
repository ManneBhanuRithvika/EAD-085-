Welcome to Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology

Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT) is a premier institute offering quality education in engineering, technology, and management. Established with a vision to provide world-class learning opportunities, CBIT emphasizes innovation, research, and holistic development of students.

Our campus hosts a range of departments including Computer Science,Information Technology, Electronics, Mechanical Engineering, and Civil Engineering. With modern laboratories, experienced faculty, and a vibrant student community, CBIT ensures an environment conducive to learning and professional growth.

We organize workshops, seminars, and industry interactions regularly to prepare students for real-world challenges. Explore our departments, join our events, and become part of the CBIT family.
