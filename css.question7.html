Welcome to CBIT

Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT) is a leading institute providing quality education in engineering, technology, and management. We focus on innovation, research, and overall student development.

Our departments include Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering. The campus is equipped with modern labs, libraries, and facilities to enhance learning and skill development.

CBIT hosts workshops, seminars, and industry interactions regularly to prepare students for professional challenges.
